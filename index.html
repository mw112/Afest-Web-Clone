About A-Fest

A-Fest is an invite-only community of 2000+ extraordinary people from 50+ countries who come together to connect, grow and learn from the world's best teachers. It takes place once a year in 5-star paradise locations across the world. These events gather an extraordinary community of change-makers and visionaries who are driven by epic ideas to impact the world – entrepreneurs, experts, artists, leaders, innovators, visionaries and more. A-Fest is like stepping into a different reality of wonder and magic. Since A-Fest is best expressed in visuals and not words, watch the After-Movie of previous events.

A-Fest Portugal

This A-Fest explores the topic of Influence, Authenticity, Messaging and Thought Leadership. We are all put on this earth to play a part in shaping a better future. The Tribe we are bringing together seeks to play big, and put a dent in the universe. This event will prepare you for the journey, and give you the tools to get there faster. You will learn to grow your following, master the tools of modern media, and find your messaging, voice and values to turn you and your business into a powerful force for change in the world.

Meet the Trainers

At A-Fest, we bring to the stage top experts from around the world to talk about the chosen theme and share their best practices, knowledge and wisdom. By the time you leave A-Fest, you will have a host of amazing ideas to unleash your extraordinary, awaken your mind, upgrade your body, and grow your business or career, so you can play a bigger and bolder game and kick ass at what you love doing.
